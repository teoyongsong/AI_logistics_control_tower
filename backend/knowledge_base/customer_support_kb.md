# Customer Support KB

## faq:delivery_eta
If you ask where a parcel is, the parcel is in transit and the expected delivery time can be shared using the latest operational estimate.

Example response: Your parcel is in transit and expected to arrive by tomorrow 18:00 local time.

## policy:reschedule
For refund/claim requests, the support flow is to guide the customer through filing a claim and the required documents.

Example response: I can guide you through filing a claim and the required documents.

## policy:handoff
For complex cases or anything that cannot be resolved through the FAQ/policy snippets, the assistant can connect the customer to a live agent.

Example response: I found relevant guidance and can connect you to a live agent for complex cases.

## policy:escalation
If the user expresses a complaint, or if confidence in retrieved guidance is below the configured threshold, escalate to a human agent.
